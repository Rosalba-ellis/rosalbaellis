CHAMPIONING THE CAUSES OF TRANSPORT WORKERS WORLDWIDE

WITH THE ITF

From fighting privatisation and automation to ensuring just transitions in climate action plans and fighting for workers’ safety at work, the ITF runs many key international campaigns.

Amidst the dehumanisation of transport workers and the exploitation of global south workforces, it was imperative for creative to focus on human stories — the demands, difficulties and wins. At Small Axe we produced digital and print content to reach disparate audiences, break down information, spotlight key issues, and strengthen networks of support.
