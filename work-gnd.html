LAUNCHING THE GREEN INDUSTRIAL REVOLUTION

WITH THE BY THE MANY

By The Many, the Labour Party’s community organising unit, worked to build strong democratic and community power in the UK.

One of their key organising focuses – the Green Industrial Revolution – needed a visual identity that drew on the tradition of labour and union design and reflected the sense of the work of the organisers: insurgent, vibrant and dynamic. At Small Axe we produced creative to reflect the passion, positivity and purpose of those fighting for a better future.
