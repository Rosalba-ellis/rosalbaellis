OPPOSING the influence of oil and arms LOBBIES On our courts

WITH DEFEND OUR JURIES

Across the UK, people are being imprisoned for taking action against the arms and oil industries who have successfully lobbied our government to influence legislation and criminalise protest.

Calling to end the unjust treatment of those acting to prevent mass loss of life and ensure defendants are able to explain their motivations in court, the exhibition of political prisoners was designed to tell the story of the corruption in our courts and the UK's complicity in the genocide of Palestinians.
